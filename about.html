About the Club

The HackerYou Accessibility Club was founded by 2 students in HackerYouâ€™s immersive bootcamp in the summer of 2016.

Initially a conversational lunch gathering, it grew to more formally structured meetings after the founders graduated.

The club aims to provide a safe learning environment for junior front-end developers looking for a solid introduction to inclusive design and web accessibility practices. Read our Code of Conduct for more information about our values and principles.

We are proud to partner with the accessibility experts at a11yTO to achieve this goal.
